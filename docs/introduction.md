# Open Manuscript Initiative

The Open Manuscript Initiative (OMI) is an open-source community project dedicated to improving scholarly publishing.

## Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality documentation, plugins, integrations, and best practices for scholarly publishing platforms.

## Supported Platforms

- Open Journal Systems (OJS)
- Open Monograph Press (OMP)
- Open Preprint Systems (OPS)
- Janeway
- DSpace
- Omeka
